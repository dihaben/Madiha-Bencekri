Welcome to My Portfolio

Hello! I'm Madiha Bencekri, a Ph.D. Assistant Research Professor in Transportation Engineering at the University of Seoul. My passion lies in crafting sustainable mobility solutions and designing smarter cities.

From optimizing shared mobility systems to pioneering accessibility improvements, I leverage data analytics, machine learning, and urban planning to bridge the gap between research and impactful real-world applications.

Research Interests & Expertise

  • Innovative transportation systems and urban planning
  • Smart city solutions focusing on sustainability
  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and spatial analysis
  • Data-driven modeling using machine learning

Recognitions & Achievements

I am honored to have received several awards, including the Distinguished Paper Award (2024). My work is featured in journals like Scientific Reports and Journal of Transport Geography, reflecting my commitment to advancing the field.
