Welcome to My Portfolio

Hello! I'm Madiha Bencekri, a Ph.D. Assistant Research Professor in Transportation Engineering at the University of Seoul. With a passion for sustainable mobility, smart cities, and innovative urban solutions, I combine advanced research with practical applications to design future-ready transportation systems.

My expertise spans data analytics, machine learning, and urban planning, with a focus on addressing real-world challenges. From optimizing shared mobility to improving accessibility, my work bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and its impact on communities.

Research Interests & Expertise

  • Innovative transportation systems and urban planning
  • Smart city solutions focusing on sustainability
  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and spatial analysis
  • Data-driven modeling using machine learning

Recognitions & Achievements

I am honored to have received several awards, including the Distinguished Paper Award (2024) and the Smart City Department Scholarship (2020). My contributions to transportation engineering have been widely recognized through published research in high-impact journals like Scientific Reports and Journal of Transport Geography.
