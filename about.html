About Me

Hello! My name is Madiha Bencekri. I hold a Ph.D. in **Transportation Engineering** with a passion for designing sustainable mobility solutions and creating smart cities. With extensive experience in both academia and industry, I bridge the gap between innovative research and real-world implementation.

My expertise lies in **transportation systems**, **urban planning**, and leveraging **data-driven approaches** like **machine learning** to address mobility challenges. I aim to develop strategies that make cities smarter, greener, and more efficient.

In addition to my work, I am deeply committed to fostering collaboration, mentoring students, and working towards **equitable urban mobility systems**. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!
